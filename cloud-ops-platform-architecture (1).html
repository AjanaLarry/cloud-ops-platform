Key design decisions

Region
ca-central-1 — Canadian data residency compliance for regulated industry roles
Two AZs
High availability by default. RDS Multi-AZ and EKS node groups require at least 2 AZs — built in from day one
NAT Gateway
Managed availability over NAT instance — no patching, automatic scaling, production-grade from the start
Private subnets
EC2 and RDS in private subnets — no direct internet exposure. Only ALB and NAT in public subnets
IaC from day one
Everything in Terraform. No manual console changes after initial build. Reproducible, auditable, environment-portable
AI layer
CloudWatch → Lambda → GPT → Slack. AI-powered incident summaries reduce mean time to diagnosis without log-parsing at 2am
